Privacy Policy

Introduction

At NoYouTubeVideo, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

Data Collection

Our extension collects minimal data, all of which is stored locally on your device:

  • Time spent on YouTube (stored locally for progress tracking)
  • Your preferred theme setting (dark/light mode)
  • Custom redirect URLs (if you set them)
  • Reflection notes (stored locally only)

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and remains on your device. This includes:

  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Usage statistics for self-reflection
  • Custom redirect URLs

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your preferences locally
  • Tabs: To manage YouTube redirects
  • youtube.com domain: To function on YouTube websites

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. When you use the redirect feature, you will be directed to your chosen website, which has its own privacy policy.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's documentation and on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@noyoutubevideo.com
